Recomended Reading

  1. "The Hampton Legion" part one and part two regimental history by Ron field - Great for understanding uniforms.
  2. "Stephen Elliott Welch of the Hampton Legion" edited by John Michael Priest - unbelievable read
  3. "Hampton Legion, Infantry CSA South Carolina Regimental Roster" said bye o.Lee Sturkey
  4. "Mary Chestnut Civil War" edited by C Vann Woodward - Great for understanding the era and mentions Wade Hampton in the Legion.
  5. "A Boot Full of Memories" by. David G Douglas Second South Carolina Cavalry - Hamptons legion Calvary and second South Carolina end up together (I believe I have not read yet.)
  6. "A Confederate Surgeons Letters to His Wife" by Spencer Glasgow Welch - 13th South Carolina unit.
  7. "Giant in Gray" by Manly Wade Wilmington.
  8. "Gentleman and Soldier" by Edward G Longacre
